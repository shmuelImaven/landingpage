iMaven Help & FAQ

General Questions

What is iMaven?

iMaven is a cutting-edge advisory platform that connects individuals with experts in various fields to offer personalized, efficient, and secure consultations enhanced by artificial intelligence.

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage, fill in the registration form, and confirm your email to activate your account.

What fields of advice does iMaven cover?

Our platform offers expert advice in financial planning, health and wellness, personal development, career coaching, and more.

Is there a fee to use iMaven?

Registering on iMaven is free. However, consultation fees vary depending on the adviser’s expertise and consultation length.

Can I access iMaven on my phone?

Yes, iMaven can be accessed via a web browser on your phone, and we also offer a mobile app available for iOS and Android devices.

User Specific

How do I choose the right adviser?

You can select an adviser based on their expertise, ratings, and reviews from other users. Our search functionality also allows you to filter advisers by specialty and available times.

How do I schedule a consultation?

After selecting an adviser, you can book a consultation by choosing a suitable time from their available slots directly on their profile.

What if I miss a scheduled session?

If you miss a session, depending on the adviser’s policy, you may reschedule or request a refund. Please check the specific terms on the adviser’s profile.

Adviser Specific

How can I become an adviser on iMaven?

Professionals interested in becoming advisers must apply through our online form, undergo a verification process, and meet specific qualifications related to their field of expertise.

How do I set my availability and rates?

Advisers can set their availability and rates through their dashboard, which allows complete control over their scheduling and fees.

How are consultations conducted?

Consultations can be conducted via voice, video call, or text, depending on what the adviser offers and what the user prefers.

Technical Support

How can I recover my password?

If you forget your password, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset it. You will need access to your registered email address.

Who do I contact for support?

Our customer support team is available 24/7 via email, live chat, and phone to assist with any issues or questions you might have.

How secure is my data on iMaven?

At iMaven, we prioritize your privacy and security. All personal information and consultation details are encrypted and securely stored.
